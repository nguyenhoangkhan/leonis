Typography

The feature of the Poppins typeface used is a sturdy text structure, built on the basis of basic shapes (square, circle, triangle). The font creates a sense of technology, professionalism, modernity, and identity with the logo. The font is decent enough, not too youthful, but still fully expresses the special personality, creating a professional feeling.
